Center of Collaboration

Partnering for Innovation, Growth & Impact

Welcome to the Center of Collaboration at ENQUEbet, the flagship Innovation Hub of Malla Reddy College of Engineering & Technology (MRCET).

We are the meeting point for students, researchers, faculty, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders who believe that great ideas grow stronger together.

Our mission is to accelerate innovation, enable startups, and foster industry-academia partnerships by providing world-class infrastructure, mentoring, and a vibrant community of makers and thinkers.

Our Vision

ENQUEbet is an initiative of MRCET's Innovation Hub, designed to foster synergy among academia, industry, and the startup ecosystem. Through shared expertise, resources, and mentorship, ENQUEbet supports ideation, prototyping, research commercialization, and capacity building.

What We Offer

  • Co-lab space & prototyping labs for joint projects
  • Regular workshops on emerging tech (AI/ML, IoT, robotics, etc.)
  • Internship & research collaboration programs
  • Startup incubation & mentorship support
  • Access to fund-raising / grant opportunities
